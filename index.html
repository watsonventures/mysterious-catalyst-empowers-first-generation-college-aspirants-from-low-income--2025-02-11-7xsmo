Our Story

Founded in 2020 by a group of first-generation college graduates, Mysterious Catalyst emerged from our personal experiences with the challenges of navigating higher education. We witnessed firsthand how limited access to resources and guidance could impact educational outcomes for talented students from low-income backgrounds.

Our mission is to democratize access to higher education by providing comprehensive support, mentorship, and resources to first-generation college aspirants. We believe that every student deserves the opportunity to pursue their educational dreams, regardless of their socioeconomic background. Through innovation, community, and dedication, we're working to create a more equitable educational landscape.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I qualify for the program?

Our program is open to all first-generation college aspirants from low-income backgrounds. You must be a current high school student or recent graduate planning to attend college.

What resources do you provide?

We offer mentorship, scholarship guidance, application support, financial literacy training, and access to a supportive community of peers and successful graduates.

Is there a cost to join?

Our core services are completely free for qualified students. We believe in removing financial barriers to accessing support and resources.

How long does the program last?

Our support extends from high school through college graduation. You'll have access to resources and mentorship throughout your educational journey.

Can I become a mentor?

Yes! We welcome successful graduates to join our mentorship program. Contact us to learn more about giving back to the community.
